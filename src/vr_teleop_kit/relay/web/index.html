Wrist calibration

Aligns the IK pivot with your real wrist so twisting a controller doesn't drift the arm.

  1. Press Start.
  2. Once in VR, squeeze both grip buttons to begin capture.
  3. Twist each hand for ~5s while keeping the wrist itself in place: the hand rotates, the wrist pivot stays still.
